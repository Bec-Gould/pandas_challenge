# HeroesOfPymoli



## Overview

As the lead analyst for an independent gaming company, analyse the data for their most recent fantasy game Heroes of Pymoli.     

## Trend 1

	*The most common players of Heroes of Pymoli are Male's aged between 20-24. Males make up over 84% of all players. 

## Trend 2

	*Players aged between 35-39, on average spend more money on their purchases than any other age group. 

## Trend 3 

	*Players aged under 10 have the second highest average total purchase per person.
